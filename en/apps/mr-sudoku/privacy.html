Privacy Policy

Last updated: 13/05/2026

This policy explains how the MR Sudoku mobile application ("the App", "we", "us") processes information when you use it.

1. Who we are

The App is provided by MR Pixel Suit Studio. For any privacy-related question, contact us at mrantonio9285@gmail.com.

2. Data we process

Data stored only on your device

The App stores preferences, game state, statistics, progress, trophies, themes and settings locally. This data remains on your device and can be deleted by uninstalling the App or clearing the app storage.

Advertising and rewarded ads

The App uses Google AdMob to show banners, interstitial ads and rewarded videos. Google and its partners may process advertising identifiers, device information, ad interactions, diagnostic data and technical data needed to deliver, measure and protect ads.

In some regions, including the European Economic Area, the United Kingdom and Switzerland, the App shows a consent form provided by Google. You can change your choices from the Information, Privacy and consent section of the App.

Google information: Google Privacy Policy and Google AdMob.

Diagnostics and analytics data

If Firebase Analytics or Firebase Crashlytics are enabled in the future, the App may send usage events, diagnostic data and crash reports to improve stability, quality and functionality. This policy will be updated before those services are enabled in production.

Problem reports by email

If you use the "Report a problem" feature, the App opens your email client with a prefilled message. You can edit or delete the content before sending it. The email is sent only if you confirm it in your email client.

3. Legal bases

For users in the European Economic Area, processing is based on:

  • Contract performance: saving settings and game state.
  • Legitimate interest: maintaining security, stability and abuse prevention.
  • Consent: where required, for personalized ads and advertising technologies.

4. Retention

Local data remains on your device until you uninstall the App or clear its data. Data processed by Google follows Google's own policies and retention periods.

5. Children

The App is intended for a general audience and does not knowingly collect personal data from children. If you believe that a child has provided us with data, please contact us.

6. Your rights

You can change advertising preferences from Information, Privacy and consent. For local data, you can clear the App data from your device settings or uninstall the App. For privacy requests, contact us by email.

7. Changes

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date shows the latest revision.

8. Contact

For any question about this policy: mrantonio9285@gmail.com.
